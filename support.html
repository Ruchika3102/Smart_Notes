Support

We're here to ensure you have the best experience with Smart Notes. Our support team is dedicated to helping you navigate the platform, resolve issues, and make the most of our resources.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I create an account?
To create an account, click the "Login/Signup" button in the navigation bar, then select "Sign Up." Fill in your details, including your email and a secure password, and follow the prompts to complete registration.
How can I upload my notes?
After logging in, navigate to the "Create" section. Follow the instructions to upload your notes, ensuring they meet our content guidelines for quality and relevance.
Why can't I find specific notes?
Try using specific keywords in the search bar. If you still can't find what you need, check the "Browse by Semester" section or contact us for assistance.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email. You'll receive a link to reset your password securely.
Is my data secure on Smart Notes?
We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

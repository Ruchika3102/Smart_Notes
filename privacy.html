Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Notes collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our website and services.

Last updated: September 5, 2025

Who we are

Smart Notes is an educational platform designed to help learners organize and manage study resources effectively. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled with the utmost care.

You can contact us at Technocrafters2gmail.com for any privacy-related concerns.

Information we collect

To provide you with the best possible experience on Smart Notes, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and securely hashed password
  • Content Data: Notes, uploaded files, and study materials you create or share
  • Usage Analytics: Device type, browser information, and platform activity logs
  • Cookies & Preferences: Used for maintaining login sessions, theme preferences, and analytics

How we use your information

Your information helps us deliver and improve our services in the following ways:

  • Provide and continuously improve Smart Notes features and functionality
  • Personalize your study experience based on your preferences and usage patterns
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform and user data
  • Communicate important updates, security alerts, and platform improvements

How we protect your data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and study materials. This includes end-to-end encryption, secure server infrastructure, multi-factor authentication, and strict access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can access user data when necessary for platform maintenance.

Your rights and control

You have full control over your data on Smart Notes. Your rights include:

  • Request access to all data we have stored about you
  • Update, modify, or delete your account information at any time
  • Export all your notes and study materials in standard formats
  • Opt out of promotional emails and disable non-essential cookies
  • Request complete account deletion with all associated data

Changes to this policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or platform features. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the platform, email, or by posting a notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Do you agree to our Privacy Policy?

By continuing to use Smart Notes, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to our Privacy Policy.
